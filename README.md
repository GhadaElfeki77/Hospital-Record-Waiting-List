# Hospital-Record-Waiting-List

Turning Healthcare Data into Actionable Insights by Power BI

📊 

In healthcare, long waiting times can directly impact patient outcomes. That’s why I worked on analyzing hospital inpatient and outpatient waiting lists to uncover trends, identify inefficiencies, and support better decision-making.

📈 Key Insights
**Current waiting list: 709K patients
**Previous year: 640K patients

📊 Increase of +69K patients (~10.8% growth)
⏱ Average waiting time: 45.38
📉 Median waiting time: 13

💡 What this means
The significant gap between the average and median indicates the presence of outliers, where some patients experience much longer waiting times — highlighting opportunities for process improvement.

💡 Impact
This project provides valuable insights for optimizing resource allocation, improving patient flow, and enhancing overall healthcare service delivery.
